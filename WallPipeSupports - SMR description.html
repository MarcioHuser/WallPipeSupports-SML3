The latest version is compatible with Update 8.2

This mod adds six new wall pipe/hypertube support variations: - One with three pipe/hypertube connections side by side - One with two pipe/hypertube connections, with gap between the two connections - One with two pipe/hypertube connections, far from the wall - One with two pipe/hypertube connections, close to the wall - One with a single pipe/hypertube connection, 6 m away from the wall - One with a single pipe/hypertube connection, 4 m away from the wall - The milestone for pipe supports will be available after you unlock "Coal Generator" - The milestone for hypertube supports will be available after you unlock "Hypertubes"

This mod is completely free. But if you wish to donate a value as a “thank you”, feel free to click on the button below. Find me at Satisfactory Modding Discord server (@MarcioHuser)
